ABOUT THE COMPANY

‘Phenotype’ is the physical manifestation of a gene code or Genotype. We believe that all design and architectural spaces are ultimately a physical response to a set of information. The site conditions and the client brief along with the program and materiality give us parameters within which we like to evolve a DNA or set of rules for the project. Our projects span across product, interiors to architecture and planning. We are able to use our methodology at various scales. The studio is a boutique practice which has completed over 75 projects till date. We continue to evolve and push ourselves in design play. We approach all projects with a pragmatic, yet playful sensibility and hope to bring joy and wellness through our work.


ANICA KOCHHAR
B.ARCH- THE PRATT INSTITUTE, NYC M.ARCH URBANISM-ARCHITECTURAL ASSOCIATION, LONDON THESIS EXHIBITTED AT THE CENTRE DE POMPEIDOU IN PARIS FOR 18MONTHS
Anica Kochhar: I knew I wanted to be an architect since I was 11. After a stint at SPA, I finally had the great honour to study architecture at the Pratt institute the in the rich and forever moving urban fabric of New York City. After my B.Arch, I returned to Delhi determined to push the design boundaries in the capital. I worked with Morphogenesis for a great 2 year stint which gave me lots of confidence in concept design and gave me a hang of how the architecture and client scene in India is. In 2008, I joined the Design Research Laboratory at the Architectural Association in London. This experience gave me great perspective on a global and culturally diverse platform. At the end of our thesis, our work on genetic architecture was displayed at the Centre Pompidou in Paris. This is where I imbibed the logic of bottom up design, not only as a hierarchy but design philosophy
