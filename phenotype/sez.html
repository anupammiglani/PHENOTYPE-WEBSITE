Corporate office, at Lotus SEZ, Bargur: This corporate campus is conceptualised as a well lit, environmentally friendly space using Indian materials in a contemporary and performance oriented way. The small building itself is made in brick and concrete, but to include the open spaces into the corporate volume we enclosed them in a second skin. This facade system is made of a metal frame hosting Bamboo which is native to the region. The panels can swivel and provide different degrees of light and privacy as required within. The entrance box is a dramatic volume in yellow which forms the reception and forces one to experience the bigger block from certain vantage points.
