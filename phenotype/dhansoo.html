We retrofitted an otherwise stiff and formal space, to make it one full of life and colour, celebrating vibrancy in Indian culture. Since the cuisine is modern Indian, we came up with a modern expression of our monuments to express this visually. These linework installations give the brand its own unique identity and keeps the people engaged while dining. Step well coffee tables and cane lights make the space edgy and relaxed at the same time.
