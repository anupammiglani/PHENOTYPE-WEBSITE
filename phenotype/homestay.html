The site sits magically on the riverbed and has a clear 360degree view. We learned a whole new construction methodology before we could construct this. Since it is in such a remote location with such dramatic weather and the harshest of winters, we have built this in a completely eco- sustainable, yet weather proof way. The Willow and Sal trees found on site have been made into columns and beams. The branches laid out form the floor structure and then mud bricks also sourced locally are arranged with an air gap to keep the building insulated from the harsh climate. The only thing sourced externally are the large windows so one can enjoy the pristine views of Shail. When complete this home will host 6 bedrooms, each one inspired by an element of nature. It even boasts a skylight since the starriest nights happen here.
